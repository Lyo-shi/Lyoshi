About Me

I'm Alexey Kozlov, a composer and musician with over 20 years of experience. My musical journey has taken me through various traditions, instruments, and philosophies that have shaped me into the composer I am today.

My path began with crafting bamboo flutes and playing guitar. I spent time in India, where I studied classical Indian music and konnakol (vocal percussion).

For many years, I worked as a jazz guitarist, exploring harmonic possibilities and improvisational freedom. This experience taught me to balance structure with spontaneity, tradition with innovation.

Currently, I'm immersed in studying Joseph Schillinger's system of composition — a revolutionary mathematical approach to musical composition that opens new horizons in my creative work.

Through this website, I share my compositions and musical sketches, documenting my creative process and growth as a composer.

Philosophy

Compassion is the most important thing in the world. I create music to evoke this feeling.

(Original Russian: Сострадание — самая важная штука в мире. Я создаю музыку, чтобы вызывать это чувство.)
